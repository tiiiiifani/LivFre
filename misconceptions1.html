Master List of Misconceptions

Tiffany Chan

Milk doesn’t make your bones stronger
There haven’t been scientific studies with strong enough results that indicate a relationship between milk and stronger bones.

Organic food isn’t necessarily pesticide free
Instead, farmers are allowed to use pesticides that are derived naturally, but may not have a good impact on the environment. Honestly between organic and not organic food, there’s a negligible amount of pesticides that it’s fine to eat either or. Just remember to wash your fruit!

There’s no difference between natural sugar and artificial sugar
Sugar in general reacts the same way to your body. Instead of caring about the type of sugar, focus on how much you intake per day.

That includes no difference between brown and white sugar
Brown sugar is just white sugar that is less refined. They both react the same to your body.

You shouldn’t drink 8 cups of water a day
Just drink water when you’re thirsty, and you’ll be fine. You should drink more if you have symptoms that indicate it like dark yellow urine. However, if you force yourself to drink more water than you need to, you may overhydrate, which is an actual thing to worry about.
